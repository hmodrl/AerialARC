When I join an online game I don't see the car that I spawned, how do I fix this?

You will first have to close the game, then go to your Documents\MyGames\RocketLeague\TAGame folder and inside there, you will have to delete the folder called "SaveDataEpic", once done restart the game and the problem will be solved.

Is NitroMOD bannable?

No, because it's all client-side, so only you will be able to see the items spawned in an online game. The modifications are local to your system and don't interfere with game servers.
